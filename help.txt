Rules:

This is a 60 seconds two player shooting game. There are two levels and the level changes after 30 seconds.
The change of level determines increase in difficulty level.

Shooting each object gives you 1 point.

Controls:

Player 1(left): 
Move up : W
Mve down: S
angle up : A
angle down : D
Increase in speed: press and hold SPACE
Shoot : Release SPACE


Player 2(right):
Move up: UP arrow key
Move Down: DOWN arrow key
angle up and angle donw controlled by mouse movement
Increase speed : Hold left mouse button
Shoot : Release Left mouse button

General:
Zoom in : z
Zoom out : x
